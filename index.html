The Challenge

Why this matters in Yobe State

🏫 Interrupted schooling

Insecurity and displacement can stop students from attending school regularly. Many learners need a safe way to continue studying from any location.

👩‍🏫 Too few teachers

One teacher may handle 100 to 200 students. This makes it hard for each learner to ask questions and get enough attention in class.

🗣️ Language gap

Some students understand Hausa better than English. Lessons with translation can improve understanding and confidence.

📶 Limited internet and materials

Students often have low data access and few textbooks. This platform uses small, fast pages and essential content only.
